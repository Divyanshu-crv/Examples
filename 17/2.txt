Question 
Ek webpage banao jisme:
1) Text ka size aur font face change ho
2) <font size=""> aur <font face=""> use ho
3) Text Hinglish me ho

Solution:
- <font size="number">Text</font> = Text ka size set karta hai (1-7)
- <font face="fontname">Text</font> = Text ka font style change karta hai
- Students ko sikhaata hai ki text ka appearance customize kaise kare
