Question:
Ek webpage banao jisme:
1) <big> tag use karke text bada dikhao
2) <small> tag use karke text chhota dikhao
3) <tt> tag use karke text typewriter style me dikhao
4) Text Hinglish me ho

Solution:
- <big> = Text ko thoda bada karta hai
- <small> = Text ko thoda chhota karta hai
- <tt> = Typewriter style text dikhata hai
- Ye tags beginners ko size aur style ka simple example sikhate hain
