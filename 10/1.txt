Question:
Ek webpage banao jisme:
1) Normal link color set ho
2) Visited link color alag ho
3) Active link color alag ho (jab click karte hain)
4) Inline CSS use ho
5) Text Hinglish me ho

Solution:
- Inline CSS me style directly <a> tag ke andar likhte hain: style="color:colorname"
- a:link = Normal link
- a:visited = Visit ke baad ka color
- a:active = Click karte waqt color
- Beginners ko sikhaata hai ki inline styling kaise hoti hai
