Question 21.2:
Ek aur webpage banao jisme:
1) UL aur OL dono use ho
2) Lists ko center align kare
3) UL ke liye different type set ho
4) OL ke liye different type set ho
5) Text Hinglish me ho

Solution:
- <ul type="square"> = Bullet style square
- <ol type="i"> = Numbering style lowercase Roman
- <center> tag se lists center me dikhegi
- Students ko sikhaata hai ki attributes se list ka appearance customize kaise kare
