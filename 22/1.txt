Question 22.1:
Ek webpage banao jisme:
1) <img> tag use ho
2) border, hspace, vspace, align attributes use ho
3) Text Hinglish me ho

Solution:
- border = Image ke around border lagata hai
- hspace = Image ke left aur right space deta hai
- vspace = Image ke top aur bottom space deta hai
- align = Image ko left, right ya center me set karta hai
- Students ko image positioning aur spacing samajhne me help milti hai
