Question:
Ek webpage banao jisme:
1) Pura body ke liye margin set ho
2) Inline CSS use ho
3) Text Hinglish me ho

Solution:
- Inline CSS me body tag me style attribute ka use karke margin set karte hain
- margin-top, margin-bottom, margin-left, margin-right = pura page ka spacing control karta hai
- Students ko sikhaata hai ki body level inline margin kaise apply kare
