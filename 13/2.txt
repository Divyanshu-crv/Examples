Question:
Ek webpage banao jisme:
1) Paragraph ke liye left, right, top, bottom margin set ho
2) Inline CSS use ho
3) Text Hinglish me ho

Solution:
- Inline CSS me style attribute ke andar margin properties likhi jaati hain
- margin-left, margin-right, margin-top, margin-bottom = individual paragraphs ka spacing control karte hain
- Students ko element level margin ka practice milega
