Question:
Ek webpage banao jisme:
1) Text ko bold (<b>) karo
2) Text ko italic (<i>) karo
3) Text ko underline (<u>) karo
4) Line break (<br>) use karo
5) Text Hinglish me ho

Solution:
- <b> = Bold text
- <i> = Italic text
- <u> = Underline text
- <br> = Nayi line start karne ke liye
- Ye tags beginners ko basic text formatting sikhaate hain
