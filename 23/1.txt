Question 23.1:
Ek webpage banao jisme:
1) <a> (anchor) tag use ho
2) href, target, title attributes use ho
3) Text Hinglish me ho

Solution:
- <a> tag = Hyperlink banane ke liye use hota hai
- href = Link ka address deta hai
- target = Link kahan open hoga (_blank, _self)
- title = Mouse hover par tooltip dikhata hai
- Students ko website navigation ka basic samajh aata hai
