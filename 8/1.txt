Question:
Ek webpage banao jisme:
1) <sub> tag use karo (subscript text)
2) <sup> tag use karo (superscript text)
3) <s> tag use karo (strike-through text)
4) Text Hinglish me ho, beginners friendly

Solution:
- <sub> = Text ko thoda niche dikhata hai (subscript)
- <sup> = Text ko thoda upar dikhata hai (superscript)
- <s>   = Text ko strike-through (line se cross) karta hai
- Ye tags students ko text formatting aur special text effects sikhaate hain
