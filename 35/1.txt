Topic: HTML5 Semantic Tags (All In One Notes)

1. <header> Tag:

Header tag website ke top part ke liye hota hai.
Isme generally logo, website ka naam, heading wagairah hoti hai.

2. <nav> Tag:

Nav tag navigation ke liye hota hai.
Isme menu links jaise Home, About, Courses, Contact hote hain.

3. <main> Tag:

Main tag webpage ke main content ko show karta hai.
Website ka sabse important content isi ke andar hota hai.

4. <section> Tag:

Section tag content ko parts me divide karta hai.
Jaise courses ka section, about ka section, etc.

5. <article> Tag:

Article tag independent content ke liye use hota hai.
Jaise blog post, news article, course detail, etc.

6. <aside> Tag:

Aside tag side content ke liye hota hai.
Jaise notice, ads, extra information.

7. <footer> Tag:

Footer tag webpage ke bottom ke liye hota hai.
Isme copyright, address, contact info hoti hai.

8. <mark> Tag:
   Mark tag text ko highlight karne ke liye use hota hai.
   Jaise kisi important word ko yellow highlight karna.

9. <details> Tag:

Details tag hide/show content ke liye hota hai.
User jab click karta hai tab andar ka content show hota hai.

10. <summary> Tag:

Summary tag, details tag ka heading hota hai.
Is par click karke details open hota hai.

11. <figure> Tag:

Figure tag image, diagram, media ko group karne ke liye hota hai.

12. <figcaption> Tag:

Figcaption tag image ka caption likhne ke liye hota hai.
Ye figure tag ke andar hota hai.

13. <abbr> Tag:
    Abbr tag short form ka full form dikhane ke liye hota hai.
    Mouse hover karne par full form show hota hai.

14. <progress> Tag:
    Progress tag progress bar dikhane ke liye hota hai.
    Jaise student ka course kitna complete hua.
